### About NNESLÄH

NGAM NTA'A E SIRR LÄH (NNESLÄH) is the Binka Military Professionals Group, a network of military and law enforcement professionals from Binka, Northwest Province, Cameroon. This organization brings together members serving in various branches:

- 🪖 **Army** - Ground forces and military operations
- ⚓ **Navy** - Naval operations and maritime defense
- 👮 **Police** - Law enforcement and public safety
- 🛡️ **Gendarmerie** - Military police and security
- 🌊 **Marine** - Marine corps operations
- 🛃 **Customs** - Border protection and trade enforcement
